Virtual Reality Tour Instructions

Navigation on map

On this screen you will see a large bird’s eye view of the BIO5 Institute tri-building complex. On this screen select the different location indicators to view in immersive Virtual Reality (VR). Each location indicator will be a VR experienc on the tour.

  • Instructions
    • Viewing a point of interest
      • With your mouse or finger click or press one of the circular blue points of interest or the “Start”/“End” point of the tour

Navigation within a scene

In a scene the method for viewing different sections of the immersive scene depend on the device that you are using to view the scene.

  • Instructions
    • Looking around in a scene
      • On computer
        • Click and drag with the mouse in the scene to view additional parts of the immersive scene
      • On Mobile
        • Orientation sensor
          • Re-orient your mobile device so it is sideways
          • Turn yourself and the phone in whatever direction you would like to see in the scene
        • Finger dragging
          • Drag a finger left or right, up or down in the scene to view additional parts of the scene
    • Exiting a scene
      • With your mouse or finger select the large “X” mark in the top left corner of the screen
      • You will be placed back at the initial map of the BIO5 Institute tri-building complex
