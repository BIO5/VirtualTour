About BIO5

Mission

BIO5 Mission and Vision Infographic

The BIO5 Institute connects and mobilizes hundreds of world-class plant, animal, and human bioscientists, engineers, physicians, and computational researchers to develop creative solutions for complex challenges such as disease, hunger, water and food safety, and other health issues facing Arizona.

This interdisciplinary approach has resulted in improved food crops, innovative diagnostics and devices, disease prevention strategies, and promising new therapies.

BIO5 initiatives and projects are carefully chosen to align with areas of state and national need and for which University of Arizona faculty already have significant expertise. This strategy catalyzes the capacity to expand impact, economic opportunity and external funding opportunities.

Virtual Reality Tour Instructions

Navigation on map

On this screen you will see a large bird’s eye view of several different floors of BIO5. This is the screen that allows you to select different locations in the building to view in immersive Virtual Reality.

  • Instructions
    • Viewing a point of interest
      • With your mouse or finger click or press one of the circular blue points of interest or the “Start”/“End” point of the tour
    • Viewing different floors
      • With your mouse or finger select one of the floor bars to the right of the map layout
      • Wait for the next page to load

Navigation within a scene

In a scene the method for viewing different sections of the immersive scene depend on the device that you are using to view the scene.

  • Instructions
    • Looking around in a scene
      • On computer
        • Click and drag with the mouse in the scene to view additional parts of the immersive scene
      • On Mobile
        • Gyroscope
          • Re-orient your mobile device so it is sideways
          • Turn yourself and the phone in whatever direction you would like to see in the scene
        • Finger dragging
          • Drag a finger left or right, up or down in the scene to view additional parts of the scene
    • Exiting a scene
      • With your mouse or finger select the large “X” mark in the top left corner of the screen
      • You will be placed back at the initial map of one of BIO5’s floors
