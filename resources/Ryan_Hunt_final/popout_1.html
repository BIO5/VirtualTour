This is our drying oven where we take soils collected from the field and effectively dry them of their moisture. Since initial measurements are taken of the wet soil, we are able to calculate the moisture content of the soils based on how much water is evaporated out of the samples. Prior to this we are able to measure things such as bulk density in the field which is a measure of how dense soil particles are packed together which can tell us about pore space.
