Navigation within a scene

In a scene, the method for viewing different sections of the immersive experience depends on the device that you are using. To look around the scene on a computer click and drag with the mouse within the scene to view additional areas. On a mobile device orientation should be horizontal for the best viewing experience. From there, turn your mobile device in whatever direction you would like to view the scene. You may also drag your finger left, right, up or down to view additional parts of the scene. To exit the scene simply close the tab that has opened.
